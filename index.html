Project Overview

A Modern Digital Storefront for Team Sports Apparel

We are building an online platform where teams can browse real uniforms, visually customize every detail on genuine product photos, manage their whole squad, and complete the purchase — all in one place.

What It Is

A custom football uniform e-commerce platform combining online shopping with a design-your-own mockup tool, built specifically for the needs of teams ordering in bulk.

Problem It Solves

Today, ordering team uniforms means endless messages, confusing sample photos, and manual player lists. Our platform replaces all that with a self-service experience that is visual, accurate, and fast.

Who It Is For

Amateur football clubs, corporate teams, school squads, sports academies, and community groups — anyone who needs matching custom uniforms delivered to their door.

Business Objectives

Clear Goals. Measurable Outcomes.

Every decision in this project is anchored on five strategic business outcomes that together transform how team apparel is sold.

01

Expand the Sales Channel

Convert an offline messaging-based workflow into a 24/7 online store that captures orders any time, from any device.

02

Deliver Confidence Through Visualization

Let customers see exactly what they will receive by placing their logos, names, and numbers directly on real product photos before they pay.

03

Shorten the Order Cycle

Automate player lists, pricing, printing fees, and order documents — reducing customer service time and eliminating miscommunication errors.

04

Build a Recognizable Brand

Establish a polished, trustworthy brand presence that sets the business apart from the typical social-media-only competitor.

05

Enable Data-Driven Growth

Capture every order, customer, and product in a structured system to unlock insights on top sellers, revenue trends, and customer behavior.

06

Scale Operations Without Chaos

Give the shop a single control center to manage products, orders, and statuses — ready to grow from tens to thousands of orders.

Key Features

Built Around What Teams Actually Need

From browsing to designing to tracking the delivery — every step is covered by focused, purposeful capabilities.

For Customers · Shopping

A Beautiful, Effortless Shopping Experience

Welcoming Homepage

A branded marketing landing page that highlights featured products, customer stories, and the unique value of custom team uniforms.

Product Catalog & Search

Browse the full uniform collection with intelligent search, category filters, and instant results — finding the right style takes seconds.

Rich Product Galleries

Each product shows multiple real photos across different angles and colorways, so customers always see the real thing.

Color Variant Selection

Customers choose from curated colorways. Switching colors instantly updates every preview while keeping their design intact.

Smart Shopping Cart

A persistent cart that remembers what customers added — even across devices — so they can pick up where they left off.

Simple Secure Checkout

A clean, guided checkout with saved shipping details and cash-on-delivery for launch — ready to plug in online payments later.

The Differentiator · Design Studio

The Visual Design Studio — Our Signature Feature

Real Photo Mockup Canvas

Customers design directly on real product photos, not generic templates, so what they see is truly what they get.

Multi-Logo Library

Upload several logos — club badge, sponsors, league patches — and drag them freely onto the uniform from any angle.

Drag, Resize & Rotate

Each design element can be moved, scaled, and rotated with natural controls — just like working in a real design tool.

Multi-Angle Preview

Design the front, back, sleeves, and shorts independently — every angle stored and ready for the production team.

Quality Guardrails

The platform detects low-quality logos and gently warns the customer, reducing disappointment after delivery.

Instant Price Updates

Total cost, printing fees, and volume discounts recalculate live as the customer adjusts the design and player count.

For Team Captains · Roster

Team Management Made Simple

Player Roster Builder

Add each player's name, jersey number, and size in one organized list — one uniform set per player, one clean summary for the shop.

Excel Paste Import

Coaches can paste their roster straight from a spreadsheet — the platform recognizes columns automatically and saves hours.

Volume Discount Nudges

Friendly prompts suggest adding a few more sets to unlock better pricing — increasing average order size naturally.

For the Shop · Admin Center

A Complete Control Center for the Shop

Orders Dashboard

See every order at a glance, filter by status, and drill into the exact design, roster, and customer details for each one.

Status Updates & Notifications

Move orders through processing, shipping, and delivered — customers automatically receive email updates at the right moments.

Bulk Product Import

Import entire supplier catalogs through a guided upload wizard — no technical skill required.

Full Product Editing

Update product info, pictures, colorways, and stock levels through an intuitive admin interface.

Production-Ready Order Files

Every custom order generates a ready-to-print player list and bundles all logo files — saving the production team real time.

Business Analytics

Track revenue, popular products, and order trends to make smarter buying, marketing, and stocking decisions.

User Experience Flow

From First Visit to Delivered Kit

The customer journey is designed to feel intuitive, reassuring, and fast — guiding every visitor from discovery to order confirmation.

1

Discover

Customers land on a warm, visual homepage that showcases popular uniforms, builds trust through testimonials, and makes the next step obvious.

2

Browse & Select

They explore the catalog with filters and search, tap a product, and see full galleries, real photos, and available color options.

3

Sign In Securely

A simple email account keeps their designs, team info, and past orders safe and easy to revisit next time.

4

Customize the Design

Customers upload their logos, position them exactly where they want, and watch the design come to life on the real jersey — from every angle.

5

Add the Team

They fill in the player roster with names, numbers, and sizes — typing directly or pasting from a spreadsheet in seconds.

6

Review & Checkout

A clean cart shows the complete order, total price, and any printing fees — with friendly nudges to unlock better discounts.

7

Confirmation & Updates

Customers receive a confirmation on screen, and automatic email updates follow as the order moves toward their doorstep.

8

Delivered & Delighted

The kit arrives exactly as visualized online — building loyalty, word-of-mouth, and repeat team orders season after season.
